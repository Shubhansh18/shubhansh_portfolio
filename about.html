Subject Profile

ID: S.GUPTA-2025 // Status: Active

My path has been a series of calculated pivots, not a straight line. Driven by an entrepreneurial spirit from a young age, I navigated a critical setback—a hand injury just before my IIT exams—that taught me the value of resilience. This experience solidified my resolve to build, not just to code.

I rediscovered my passion for development by solving real-world problems, a journey that took me through a creative detour into graphic design and back to engineering with a renewed perspective. Today, I leverage this unique blend of analytical and creative skills to build intelligent, full-stack applications as both a freelancer and an aspiring founder.

Career Trajectory

  • Crossroads & Setback // The Unplanned Pivot

    A severe hand injury before my 12th board exams derailed my plans for IIT. This challenging period taught me resilience and forced me to adapt, leading me to pursue a B.Tech in CS with a new ambition: entrepreneurship.

  • The Creative Detour // Burnout & Re-Skilling

    Frustrated by the tedious aspects of early coding, I took a detour into graphic design and video editing, mastering tools like Photoshop & Premiere Pro. This wasn't a distraction; it was an unintentional discovery of my passion for user experience and visual communication.

  • The Proving Ground // Walkover Web Solutions

    Returning to code with a fresh perspective, I secured a pre-placement offer at a product-based company. Here, I fell in love with coding again—not as a chore, but as a tool for solving real-world problems.

  • The Leap // The Startup Journey

    After two years of valuable experience and learning from a side-project (Entuitor), I resigned to pursue my entrepreneurial goals full-time. Today, I'm building my next venture while freelancing to sustain the journey and solve new, exciting challenges.
