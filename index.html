IdeaVisor

AI-Powered Business Idea Validator

The Problem

The Solution

IdeaVisor is a SaaS application that acts as an AI-powered business consultant. Users input their business idea, and the tool leverages generative AI to provide a multi-faceted analysis, including Founder-market fit analysis, competitor insights, and potential challenges.

My Role

Automated LinkedIn Content

AI-Powered Content Research & Publishing Pipeline

The Problem

Maintaining a consistent, high-quality social media presence is time-consuming. The daily grind of researching trending topics, writing engaging posts, and creating visuals detracts from core development tasks.

The Solution

I built an end-to-end automated content pipeline. The system uses a self-hosted n8n workflow to research trending topics and generate post ideas with AI. A second Make.com workflow then creates a relevant image and queues the post for a simple, one-click approval in a Google Sheet. Once approved, the system automatically publishes the final post to LinkedIn.

My Role

I single-handedly designed and built the entire automation architecture, integrating multiple platforms (n8n, Make.com) and AI models to create a seamless, hands-off content generation system with a human-in-the-loop for final quality control.

Tech Stack

n8n.io Make.com Google Gemini AI Google Sheets App Scripts Google Cloud LinkedIn API

Workflow Architecture

1. Daily idea generation workflow in n8n.

2. Image creation & posting workflow in Make.com.
